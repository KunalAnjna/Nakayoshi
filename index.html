How a Build Runs

Six stages, one engineering team, start to finish.

The same crew that scopes the project is the one standing on your floor during commissioning.

01

Concept & Feasibility

We study your part, your space, and your throughput target before drawing anything.

02

Design & Simulation

3D design and cycle-time simulation catch interference and bottlenecks before steel is cut.

03

Fabrication

Frames, fixtures, and mechanisms are built and dry-fit in our shop, not on your floor.

04

Controls Integration

PLC, HMI, sensors, and safety circuits are wired, programmed, and bench-tested as one system.

05

Testing & Commissioning

Full run-off at rate, then on-site install, alignment, and operator handover.

06

Support & Maintenance

Spares, remote diagnostics, and scheduled service keep the line running for years.
